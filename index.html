 Andrew Branch
Design Director at Rev.com

Hi, I’m Jacob Ottesen. I currently work at IBM as a UX designer in Austin, TX. I’m currently working on the team that’s building a design system for all digital experiences on IBM.com.

Why the podcast?

I started this podcast as an opportunity for myself and others to learn from seasoned design experts on how we can discover our niche or speciality in design. I hope that this podcast can help you navigate your design career more clearly.
